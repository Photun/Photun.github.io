Request account deletion.

NoteLink uses anonymous app accounts. To help us find the correct account, include the name and instrument shown in your app settings, plus your band name or join code if you know it.

Email deletion request

What will be deleted?

Account deletion removes the app profile associated with the matching NoteLink account, including stored name, instrument, preferred part, and band membership. Music files and band-wide content are not deleted unless separately requested by a band owner or administrator.
